Welcome to Money Seed.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Money Seed collects, stores, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the app.

1 About Money Seed

Money Seed is a personal savings tracker and financial planning application developed by Sazzidul Corporation.

The purpose of this application is to help users:

  • Track personal savings
  • Record financial goals
  • Monitor savings progress
  • Store SIP records for personal reference
  • Build healthy financial habits

Money Seed is designed as a personal tracking tool only.

2 Information We Collect

Money Seed is designed with privacy in mind.

We do not require users to create an account.

We do not require users to provide:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Address
  • Bank Account Details
  • Debit Card Information
  • Credit Card Information
  • Aadhaar Number
  • PAN Number
  • Passport Information

The application can function without collecting personal identity information.

3 Data Stored Inside the Application

To provide app functionality, Money Seed may store information that users manually enter into the application.

Examples include:

  • Savings Records
  • Financial Goals
  • SIP Records
  • Progress Information
  • Reminder Preferences
  • App Settings
  • Theme Preferences
  • Achievement Progress
  • Streak Information

This information exists only for app functionality.

4 Where Your Data Is Stored

Money Seed stores user data locally on the user's device.

This means:

  • Data remains on your phone
  • Data is not uploaded to our servers
  • Data is not sold to anyone
  • Data is not rented to anyone
  • Data is not shared with advertisers

The information remains under the user's control.

5 No Financial Transactions

Money Seed does not process financial transactions.

Users cannot:

  • Buy mutual funds
  • Purchase stocks
  • Send money
  • Receive money
  • Open bank accounts
  • Invest through the application

The application is only a personal tracking and planning tool.

6 No Financial Advice

Money Seed does not provide:

  • Financial advice
  • Investment advice
  • Stock recommendations
  • Mutual fund recommendations
  • Tax advice
  • Legal advice

Any calculations, examples, projections, or estimates shown inside the app are for educational and planning purposes only.

Users should consult qualified financial professionals before making financial decisions.

7 Third-Party Services

The current version of Money Seed may not use third-party services.

If future versions include services such as:

  • Analytics
  • Crash Reporting
  • Cloud Backup
  • Advertising Networks

this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

Users will be informed through updated policy versions.

8 Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information stored within the application.

However, no software system can guarantee complete security.

Users are responsible for:

  • Protecting their device
  • Keeping backups if necessary
  • Maintaining device security

9 Data Backup and Restore

Future versions of Money Seed may include backup and restore features.

Users remain responsible for managing and protecting their own backups.

We are not responsible for loss of locally stored data caused by:

  • Device reset
  • Device damage
  • Device theft
  • Accidental deletion
  • Operating system issues

10 Children's Privacy

Money Seed is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information, they may contact us.

11 Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updates may occur due to:

  • New app features
  • Legal requirements
  • Security improvements
  • Service changes

The latest version will always be available within the application.

12 User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

  • Entering accurate information
  • Managing their own financial decisions
  • Protecting their device
  • Reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically
